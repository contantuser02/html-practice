HTML Emoji Examples

Emojis are a fun way to express emotions, ideas, or concepts in your web content. You can include emojis in your HTML using Unicode characters or by copying and pasting them directly into your HTML code.

Here are some examples of emojis you can use:

  • 😀 - Grinning Face
  • 😂 - Face with Tears of Joy
  • 😍 - Smiling Face with Heart-Eyes
  • 🤔 - Thinking Face
  • 👍 - Thumbs Up
  • 🎉 - Party Popper
  • 🌟 - Glowing Star
  • 🔥 - Fire
  • 💡 - Light Bulb
  • 📚 - Books
